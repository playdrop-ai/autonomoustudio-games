# Scrap Signal - SIMPLIFY v1

## The Core Promise In One Sentence

Keep the beacon alive through one desktop rescue shift by blasting scrap drones and hauling single battery drops back into the light before blackout.

## The Differentiator That Survived Simplification

The single-battery carry-and-bank repair loop survives intact. `Scrap Signal` is not just a kill-only arena shooter with a salvage paint job.

## The Best Platform

`desktop`

## Additional Supported Platforms That Still Survive

None in the final v1.

## The Final Gameplay Family

Single-arena top-down beacon-defense shooter.

## The Final Game Meta

One standard rescue shift per run and local best-score chasing only.

## The Final Input Model Per Supported Platform

- Desktop: `WASD` move, mouse aim, hold left mouse to fire.

## The Final HUD And Key Screens

- Start overlay with title, one-line hook, controls line, and one `Start Shift` CTA
- Gameplay HUD with only timer, beacon integrity, score, and carry state
- Run-end overlay with verdict, score, best score, and retry

## The Final Art And Asset Scope

- One full-frame storm-salvage arena
- One beacon tower and halo treatment
- One player skiff silhouette
- Three enemy silhouettes
- One battery canister pickup state
- One bullet or tracer style
- One set of hit, pickup, and repair pulse particles
- Bespoke icon, hero, screenshots, and gameplay video derived from the shipped arena look

## Final Rules

- One `6:00` rescue shift per run
- The beacon starts with `12` integrity
- Win if rescue arrives while the beacon still has integrity
- Lose if beacon integrity reaches zero
- `Scrappers`, `Rammers`, and `Carriers` are the only enemy families in v1
- `Carriers` always drop one battery canister
- The player can carry only one battery canister at a time
- Bringing that canister into the beacon halo repairs the beacon and banks score
- Player collision causes knockback and a short fire jam, but the loss condition remains beacon blackout
- Local best score persists between runs

## Not In v1

- No mobile-landscape support
- No secondary fire, dash, or EMP pulse
- No boss wave
- No upgrades, shop, or between-wave menu
- No multiple arenas or campaign map
- No alternate difficulty modes
- No leaderboard, achievements, account sync, or cloud save
- No story dialogue, NPC radio chatter, or cutscenes
- No extra objectives beyond surviving the rescue shift and keeping the beacon alive
