# Scrap Signal Listing Copy

## Display Name

Scrap Signal

## One-line Value Proposition

Blast scrap drones, ferry one battery at a time, and keep the rescue beacon alive through a storm-lashed salvage shift.

## Description

Move with `WASD`.
Aim with the mouse and hold left click to fire.

Scrap drones drop amber battery canisters. You can carry `1` at a time, and returning it to the beacon repairs the signal and boosts your score.

Watch the edge telegraphs so you know what is about to enter the arena and where it will arrive.

Survive until the rescue timer ends. If the beacon blacks out first, the shift is over.
