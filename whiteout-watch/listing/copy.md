# Whiteout Watch Listing Copy

## Display Name

Whiteout Watch

## One-line Value Proposition

Spend limited battery pulses on the right system before the next whiteout hits and the station goes dark.

## Description

Tap a system panel to spend 1 battery pulse and restore it.

Keep `Heat`, `Power`, and `Comms` alive as the storm escalates.

Low `Heat` increases drain across the whole station. Low `Power` slows pulse recharge. Low `Comms` scrambles the next storm forecast.

Survive as long as you can. If any system hits `0`, the shift is over.
