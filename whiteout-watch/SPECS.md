# Whiteout Watch - High-Level Requirements

- Ship a portrait-first crisis-triage game on the simple PlayDrop TypeScript app path.
- Keep the game focused on one screen, one endless mode, and one-thumb restoration taps.
- The player must keep `Heat`, `Power`, and `Comms` alive through escalating whiteout events.
- `Heat`, `Power`, and `Comms` must have meaningful interdependence:
  - low heat increases global drain
  - low power slows pulse recharge
  - low comms degrades the storm forecast
- The layout must feel like a premium full-screen storm dashboard, not a webpage card.
- Support `mobile portrait` first and keep `desktop` only if it survives without extra UI bloat.
- Avoid progression systems, currencies, extra tools, story scenes, or rescue-through-features scope.
- Raw gameplay screenshots and the first `15` seconds must already look worth clicking before listing treatment.
