# Lunchline - SIMPLIFY v1

## The Core Promise In One Sentence

Tap the right ingredient clusters to finish lunchbox orders before the shift racks up three complaints.

## The Differentiator That Survived Simplification

The active lunchbox order still changes which clusters matter from the very first move. The game is not just a generic cluster clearer with food art.

## The Best Platform

`mobile portrait`

## Additional Supported Platforms That Still Survive

- `desktop`

## The Final Gameplay Family

Endless cluster-tap order puzzler.

## The Final Game Meta

Single endless shift with best-score persistence and order-streak chasing.

## The Final Input Model Per Supported Platform

- Mobile portrait: tap a connected ingredient cluster of `2+` matching pieces.
- Desktop: click a connected ingredient cluster of `2+` matching pieces.

## The Final HUD And Key Screens

- Start overlay with title, one-line hook, and play button
- Gameplay HUD with only score, complaints, and one active lunchbox order
- Run-end overlay with score, best score, orders served, and restart

## The Final Art And Asset Scope

- One portrait board
- `4` ingredient token families
- One lunchbox order-card treatment
- One complaint/patience danger language
- Small pop, fill, and timeout effects
- Bespoke icon, hero, screenshots, and gameplay video derived from the live art direction

## Final Rules

- Ingredient family count reduced from `5` to `4`
- One active order only
- Each order asks for exactly `2` ingredient families
- Valid move:
  - tap or click a connected cluster of `2+` matching ingredients
- Each valid clear:
  - removes the cluster
  - collapses the board
  - refills from the top
- Matching needed ingredients:
  - fill the current lunchbox counts
  - award the main score value
- Off-order ingredients:
  - are still allowed for board control
  - award only a small score value
- Completing the order:
  - scores a bonus
  - refreshes patience
  - increases the streak
  - rolls the next order
- Timing out an order:
  - adds `1` complaint
  - resets the streak
  - rolls the next order
- Lose at `3` complaints

## Not In v1

- No mobile-landscape support
- No multiple simultaneous orders
- No customer queue panel
- No level map, upgrade tree, unlockables, or currency
- No powerups, bombs, reshuffle consumables, or boosters
- No authored campaign or puzzle list
- No separate combo meter beyond the order streak
- No online features, achievements, or leaderboards in the first ship
