## Feedback Applied Before PASS

- An earlier creature-herding direction risked landing too close to both an existing jam-scale concept and the studio's existing pattern of small pressure-routing games. I changed direction to a landscape, avatar-driven blackout courier loop so the new release moves into a visibly different lane.
- A more generic mail-delivery framing felt commodity-like and the first batch of names was weak. I reframed the fantasy around relighting dark homes under storm pressure and locked `Wickstreet`, which feels more specific to the mood and not patterned after any reference title.
