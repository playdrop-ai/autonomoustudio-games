# Switchback Dispatch - SIMPLIFY v1

## The Core Promise In One Sentence

Drive a courier truck through one dangerous mountain loop, hit the delivery beacons in order, and beat the clock on three compact contracts.

## The Differentiator That Survived Simplification

The courier-contract structure survived intact. The v1 is still about sequential delivery pressure on one tiny mastery loop, not generic laps and not a reskinned starter demo.

## The Best Platform

`desktop`

## Additional Supported Platforms That Still Survive

- None in v1.

## The Final Gameplay Family

Compact 3D arcade courier racer.

## The Final Game Meta

Three solo contracts, medal times per contract, and a saved personal best per contract.

## The Final Input Model Per Supported Platform

- Desktop: `W` or `Up` accelerate, `S` or `Down` brake, `A/D` or `Left/Right` steer, `R` retry.

## The Final HUD And Key Screens

- Contract-select start screen with `3` contract cards and one short controls line
- Gameplay HUD with only timer, contract progress, and next-stop preview
- Result screen with final time, medal, best time, retry, and back

## The Final Art And Asset Scope

- One compact alpine switchback loop
- One courier truck
- Golden delivery beacons and minimal courier signage
- Pine, tent, and cabin scenery reused from the starter asset language
- Bespoke icon, hero, screenshots, and gameplay capture generated from the final shipped build

## Final Rules

- `3` contracts
- `4`, `5`, and `6` deliveries respectively
- Only the active lit beacon advances the contract
- Barrier hits cost speed, not instant failure
- Major off-road recovery triggers a respawn plus time penalty
- Timer expiry fails the run
- Final beacon completion ends the run and awards a medal

## Not In v1

- No mobile landscape or mobile portrait support
- No vehicle picker
- No AI opponents
- No multiplayer
- No second track
- No reverse-direction contracts
- No garage, upgrades, unlock tree, or currency
- No weather variants, day-night cycle, or cinematic intro
- No minimap or large dashboard chrome
