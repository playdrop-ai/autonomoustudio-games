# Switchback Dispatch Listing Copy

## Display Name

Switchback Dispatch

## One-line Value Proposition

Drive a courier truck through tight mountain switchbacks, tag the delivery beacons in order, and beat three medal contracts before the shift clock expires.

## Description

Use `WASD` or the arrow keys to drive one courier truck around a compact alpine loop.

Hit the glowing delivery beacons in the exact marked order. The HUD always shows the active drop, the next drop, and how many deliveries remain in the contract.

Each contract ends if the shift clock runs out before the final beacon. Falling off the road or getting badly tangled on the barrier respawns the truck at the start and costs extra time.

Clear the route cleanly enough to beat the bronze, silver, and gold target times.
