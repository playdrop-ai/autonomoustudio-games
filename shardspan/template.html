Desktop Relay

Shardspan

Touch the relays in order. Each relay flips which bridge color is solid. Falling rewinds you to the last cleared relay and burns 4 seconds.

    Controls

    • Move WASD or arrow keys
    • Camera drag the mouse
    • Jump Space
    • Goal relays I through IV, then the exit gate

    Relay Rules

    • Amber or cobalt shows which bridges are solid now
    • Next cue points to the live target and what it changes
    • Timer starts on your first move, not on the button press
    • Falls respawn you at the last relay with a 4s penalty
