# Velvet Arcana Listing Copy

## Display Name

Velvet Arcana

## One-line Value Proposition

Read three candlelit spreads, chain exact higher-or-lower omen cards, and chase one flawless solitaire run.

## Description

Tap a face-up omen card that is exactly one rank higher or lower than the active card.

`A` and `K` wrap, so they count as neighbors.

Read the face-up next draw before you tap the stock. When the reserve charm is charged, tap it to stash the active card or swap the stored card back into play.

Finish a chain on the spread's omen suit to score a bigger bonus and recharge the reserve charm.

Clear all three spreads before the stock burns out to complete a flawless reading.
