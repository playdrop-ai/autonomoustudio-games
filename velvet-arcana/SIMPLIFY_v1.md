# Velvet Arcana - SIMPLIFY v1

## The Core Promise In One Sentence

Tap higher-or-lower omen cards through three short candlelit spreads, read the face-up next draw, use one reserve charm at the right moment, and finish a full reading before the stock runs dry.

## The Differentiator That Survived Simplification

The game keeps exactly two differentiators beyond the proven solitaire rule: a visible reserve charm and a three-spread run with one omen-suit bonus per spread. Everything else is cut.

## The Best Platform

`mobile portrait`

## Additional Supported Platforms That Still Survive

- `desktop`

## The Final Gameplay Family

Portrait solitaire card run-builder.

## The Final Game Meta

Three spreads per run, cumulative score, local best score.

## The Final Input Model Per Supported Platform

- Mobile portrait: tap a playable card, the stock, or the reserve charm.
- Desktop: click a playable card, the stock, or the reserve charm.

## The Final HUD And Key Screens

- Start overlay with title, hook, and `Play`
- Gameplay view with score, spread label, omen suit chip, reserve charm, active card well, and a face-up next-draw preview
- Run-end overlay with final score, best score, spreads cleared, and retry

## The Final Art And Asset Scope

- One full-screen velvet table background
- `52` functional card faces built from numerals and four suit icons
- One card back
- One reserve-charm emblem
- Simple glow and ember effects
- Bespoke listing icon, hero pair, screenshots, and gameplay video built from the shipped table art

## Final Rules

- `3` spreads per run
- `21` face-up tableau cards per spread in `3 x 7`
- `14` stock cards per spread after the opening active card
- The next stock draw stays face up throughout play
- Play a card only if it is exactly `+1` or `-1` from the active rank
- `A` and `K` wrap
- One reserve charm slot, charged at the start of each spread
- Using the reserve charm supports one stash or swap until it is refilled
- Ending a chain of `3+` cards on the spread's omen suit awards a bonus and refills the reserve charm
- Clear all tableau cards to finish the spread
- The run ends when no legal move remains and backup tools are exhausted

## Not In v1

- No daily challenge, campaign, or unlock map
- No alternate decks, themes, or suit sets
- No undo stack
- No hint button
- No achievements, leaderboards, or account progression
- No mobile landscape support
- No major arcana characters, illustrated face cards, or elaborate story text
- No between-spread shop, boon selection, or meta upgrade system
- No extra settings surface beyond mute and retry
