Switchyard Sprint is a toy-train routing arcade game for desktop and mobile where trains stream into a tiny three-exit rail yard and the player flips three switches in real time to send each color-coded cargo to the matching depot. The look is a bright tabletop diorama using Playdrop train references from `playdrop/asset-pack/modular-train-repack` and `kenneynl/asset-pack/train-kit`, with implementation grounded in the fixed-camera Three.js patterns from `playdrop/app/three-demo` and the fast readable mobile pacing of `playdrop/app/starter-kit-match-3`.
