# Overvolt - SIMPLIFY v1

## The Core Promise In One Sentence

Outdrive and ram rival RC cars in a bright tabletop arena to steal the battery charge that keeps your own run alive.

## The Differentiator That Survived Simplification

Only two differentiators survive beyond the proven battle-car loop:

- battery is both health and aggression reward
- the arena reads as a bright tabletop toy fight instead of a race track or weapon shooter

## The Best Platform

`mobile landscape`

## Additional Supported Platforms That Still Survive

- `desktop`

## The Final Gameplay Family

Landscape direct-control vehicle survival brawler.

## The Final Game Meta

One endless run, local best score, no progression layer.

## The Final Input Model Per Supported Platform

- Mobile landscape: left virtual stick to steer, right `Dash` button
- Desktop: `WASD` or arrow keys to steer, `Space` to dash

## The Final HUD And Key Screens

- Start overlay with title, one short how-to-play block, and `Play`
- Gameplay view with battery, score, dash cooldown, and touch controls on mobile
- Run-end overlay with score, time, best, and retry

## The Final Art And Asset Scope

- One tabletop arena
- One player car silhouette
- Two enemy silhouettes at most:
  - basic rival
  - heavy rival
- Blue battery pickups
- A few simple props:
  - rails
  - cones
  - boxes
  - one or two desk accents
- Sparks, skid streaks, and simple electric glow

## Final Rules

- Battery drains over time
- Battery drains faster when the player takes hits
- Destroyed rivals drop blue battery shards
- Collecting shards restores charge and score
- Dash gives a short burst and stronger knockback
- Basic rivals body-check aggressively
- Heavy rivals appear later and punish sloppy angles
- The run ends when battery reaches `0`

## Not In v1

- No laps
- No racing positions
- No guns or projectile weapons
- No track editor
- No multiplayer
- No upgrade tree
- No daily mode
- No multi-arena campaign
- No jump ramps or vertical track pieces
- No deep prop clutter beyond what the raw screenshot needs
- No account progression, achievements, or leaderboard integration
