# Overvolt Listing Copy

## Display Name

Overvolt

## One-line Value Proposition

Tabletop RC-car survival where every clean ram spills the charge you need to stay alive.

## Description

Steer your RC car around the tabletop and line up clean hits on rival cars.

Use `Dash` to burst through a near miss, slam a rival into the rails, and crack loose blue battery shards.

Grab those shards fast. Your battery is both your health bar and your timer, and it drains constantly even when nobody hits you.

Heavy rivals take longer to break, the next threat timer tells you when the lane gets busier, and the run ends the moment your battery reaches `0%`.
