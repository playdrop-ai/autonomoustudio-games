# Powder Post - SIMPLIFY v1

## The Core Promise In One Sentence

Carve downhill through the right village gates and keep the avalanche off your back.

## The Differentiator That Survived Simplification

The run still depends on reading the active parcel crest and choosing a matching gate line under pressure. The game is not just downhill obstacle avoidance.

## The Best Platform

`mobile landscape`

## Additional Supported Platforms That Still Survive

- `desktop`

## The Final Gameplay Family

Endless downhill slalom runner.

## The Final Game Meta

Single endless run with best-score persistence and delivery-streak chasing.

## The Final Input Model Per Supported Platform

- Mobile landscape: drag horizontally anywhere on the screen to steer.
- Desktop: steer with `A` / `D`, `Left` / `Right`, or pointer drag.

## The Final HUD And Key Screens

- Start overlay with title, one-line hook, and play button
- Gameplay HUD with only score, active parcel crest, one next preview, and avalanche distance
- Run-end overlay with score, best score, longest streak, and restart

## The Final Art And Asset Scope

- One full-screen downhill slope
- One courier silhouette with scarf/satchel accent
- `3` district crest families and matching gate styles
- Trees, rocks, and one cracked-ice obstacle family
- Powder spray, delivery spark, and avalanche surge particles
- Bespoke icon, hero, screenshots, and gameplay video derived from the live art direction

## Final Rules

- District count reduced from `4` to `3`
- Parcel preview reduced from `2` upcoming crests to `1`
- Matching gate:
  - scores
  - advances the parcel queue
  - increases the streak
  - pushes the avalanche back
- Wrong gate:
  - resets the streak
  - advances the queue
  - pulls the avalanche closer
- Missing a gate:
  - keeps the same parcel active
  - gives no score
  - lets the avalanche continue to advance
- Tree, rock, or cracked-ice collision:
  - causes a short stumble
  - resets the streak
  - advances the avalanche the most
- Speed rises in small tiers after repeated successful deliveries
- Lose when the avalanche reaches the courier

## Not In v1

- No mobile portrait support
- No between-run upgrades, currencies, or progression tree
- No campaign map, authored level list, or chalet hub
- No trick system, jump button, boost button, or combat
- No online features, ghosts, or multiplayer
- No weather variants beyond the core storm pressure
- No separate pause/settings surface beyond mute and restart
- No extra HUD widgets beyond the minimal score, parcel, streak, and avalanche information
