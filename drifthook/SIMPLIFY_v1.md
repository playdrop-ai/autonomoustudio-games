# Drifthook - SIMPLIFY v1

## The Core Promise In One Sentence

Lower a lantern lure into dark water, reel up the leftmost target fish next, and keep your four-knot line from fraying out.

## The Differentiator That Survived Simplification

The three-slot target order card and knot-repair economy survived untouched, so the v1 is still about deliberate order-driven fishing under pressure rather than catch-anything spectacle or a theme-swapped timing toy.

## The Best Platform

`mobile portrait`

## Additional Supported Platforms That Still Survive

- `desktop`

## The Final Gameplay Family

Single-screen fishing timing arcade.

## The Final Game Meta

One endless score run with best-score persistence and completed-order-count persistence.

## The Final Input Model Per Supported Platform

- Mobile portrait: hold to lower the lure and release to reel it back up.
- Desktop: hold `Space` or mouse-down to lower and release to reel.

## The Final HUD And Key Screens

- Start overlay with title, one short how-to-play line, and `Play`
- Gameplay HUD with only score, four knot pips, and the three-slot order strip
- Game-over overlay with score, best, completed orders, and `Retry`

## The Final Art And Asset Scope

- One full-screen moonlit lake cross-section
- One lantern lure and one centered line path
- `4` fish silhouettes with distinct swim patterns
- One snag family built from kelp and drift silhouettes
- Bubbles, soft wake ripples, and small catch particles
- Bespoke icon, hero, screenshots, and gameplay capture built from the live scene

## Final Rules

- `4` fish species
- `3` target fish per order card
- `4` line knots max
- Hold lowers the lure; release reels it up
- The first fish or snag touched during the ascent resolves the reel
- Correct target catch fills the next order slot and scores
- Perfect center catch scores extra
- Wrong fish or snag costs `1` knot and resets order progress to slot `1`
- Completing an order grants a big score bonus and repairs `1` knot
- Every `3` completed orders increases fish speed and snag pressure
- Lose at `0` knots

## Not In v1

- No mobile landscape support
- No cast-angle control or horizontal lure steering
- No inventory, upgrades, shop, currency, or unlock tree
- No different lakes, day-night cycle, or campaign map
- No achievements, leaderboards, multiplayer, or account-gated saves
- No rare collectible fish book or long tutorial screens
- No more than one snag family in the shipped build
