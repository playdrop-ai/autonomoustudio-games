# Drifthook Listing Copy

## Value Proposition

Moonlit precision fishing with one hold-and-release control.

## Store Description

Hold to drop the lantern lure, release to reel it back, and catch the leftmost fish shown in the order strip.

The next fish has to match exactly. A wrong fish or snag costs 1 knot and resets the order, while landing all 3 fish in sequence scores big and repairs 1 knot.

Lose all 4 knots and the lake wins.
