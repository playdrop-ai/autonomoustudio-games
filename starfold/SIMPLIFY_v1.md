# Starfold - SIMPLIFY v1

## The Core Promise In One Sentence

Swipe whole rows and columns of glowing sigils to trigger chain reactions while keeping ash from overrunning the board.

## The Differentiator That Survived Simplification

Whole-line row and column shifts are the primary interaction, and ash blockers create light board-control pressure without adding extra systems or meta layers.

## The Best Platform

`mobile portrait`

## Additional Supported Platforms That Still Survive

- `desktop`

## The Final Gameplay Family

Single-screen arcade puzzle.

## The Final Game Meta

One-run score chase with best-score persistence.

## The Final Input Model Per Supported Platform

- Mobile portrait: one swipe shifts exactly one row or column by one cell.
- Desktop: one mouse drag does the same.

## The Final HUD And Key Screens

- Minimal start overlay
- Active run HUD with only score and ash meter
- Run-end overlay with score, best, and restart

## The Final Art And Asset Scope

- One canvas playfield
- Five sigil designs
- One ash blocker design
- Simple particle bursts and glow pulses
- Bespoke SVG-based icon, hero, and screenshot framing

## Final Rules

- `5 x 6` board
- `5` sigil types
- Match orthogonal groups of `3+`
- Shift one row or one column by one cell per move
- Gravity refill after clears
- Every fourth move adds one ash blocker
- Adjacent clears cleanse ash
- Lose at `10` ash blockers

## Not In v1

- No extra game modes
- No level map or campaign
- No currency, achievements, or progression tree
- No special power tiles
- No online features or account-gated saves
- No mobile landscape support
- No tutorial screen beyond a short opening overlay
