# Relayline Listing Copy

## Display Name

Relayline

## One-line Value Proposition

Use clue-count logic to carve a live circuit from the source to the relay before the third surge burns the board.

## Description

Tap a covered cell to reveal it. Turn on Flag Mode to mark a short you want to avoid.

Each number works like a minefield clue: it tells you how many adjacent cells hide shorts.

You do not need to clear the whole board. Win when a revealed safe route connects the gold source in the top-left to the cyan relay in the bottom-right.

Hit 3 shorts and the circuit burns out.

Choose Warm, Live, or Deep depending on how much board you want to route through.
