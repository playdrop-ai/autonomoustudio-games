# Relayline - SIMPLIFY v1

## The Core Promise In One Sentence

Use familiar clue logic to reveal a safe glowing route from source to relay before three overload surges end the board.

## The Differentiator That Survived Simplification

The source-to-relay route objective and the visible lit-current payoff survive intact. `Relayline` is not just a numbered minefield with a different wrapper.

## The Best Platform

`mobile portrait`

## Additional Supported Platforms That Still Survive

- `desktop`

## The Final Gameplay Family

Single-board generated deduction route puzzler.

## The Final Game Meta

Pick one of three difficulties, solve one generated board, and chase better times locally.

## The Final Input Model Per Supported Platform

- Mobile portrait: tap to reveal, tap the persistent flag chip and then tap a cell to flag or unflag it.
- Desktop: left-click to reveal, right-click to flag, or press `F` for flag mode.

## The Final HUD And Key Screens

- Start overlay with title, one-line hook, and three difficulty buttons
- Gameplay HUD with only timer, `3` surge pips, and the flag chip
- Run-end overlay with result, clear time, best time, and retry

## The Final Art And Asset Scope

- One full-frame board treatment
- Covered, revealed, lit, flagged, and burnt cell states
- Source node and relay node
- Clean clue-number set
- One zero-cascade treatment
- One minimal start overlay and one run-end overlay
- Bespoke icon, hero, screenshots, and gameplay video based on the shipped board look

## Final Rules

- `3` difficulty presets:
  - `Warm`: `8 x 10`, `10` shorts
  - `Live`: `9 x 13`, `20` shorts
  - `Deep`: `10 x 16`, `32` shorts
- Source and relay are always visible and always safe
- Tap or click a covered cell to reveal it
- A safe reveal shows a standard `0-8` clue count
- Revealing `0` floods outward through safe neighbors
- Flagged cells cannot be revealed until unflagged
- Revealing a shorted cell burns it and consumes one surge
- Current travels only through orthogonally adjacent revealed safe cells
- Revealed safe cells light up when connected to the source network
- Win when the lit network reaches the relay
- Lose on the third overload surge
- Keep local best times per difficulty only

## Not In v1

- No mobile-landscape support
- No daily board
- No campaign, chapter map, or multi-board adventure
- No hint button, auto-solver, or undo stack
- No combo score system beyond clear time and best time
- No alternate themes, skins, or visual modes
- No account sync, achievements, leaderboard, or cloud save
- No story wrapper, character art, or extra decorative scene dressing
- No extra tools beyond reveal and explicit flagging
