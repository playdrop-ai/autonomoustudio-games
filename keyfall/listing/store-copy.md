## Name

Keyfall

## Short Description

Tap the lane. Hold the long tile. Hit doubles together.

## Value Proposition

Minimal 3D rhythm lane.
Portrait first.
Keyboard ready.

## How To Play

- Tap the exact lane when the tile reaches the strike line.
- Hold the long tile until it ends.
- Hit double tiles on both lanes together.
- Misses cost lives.
- Stay alive and push the score.
